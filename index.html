The Challenge

Why Emerging Market Lending Stalls

For commodity and energy companies in emerging markets, access to credit remains constrained — not by opportunity, but by opacity.

Information Asymmetry

Lenders lack standardised frameworks to evaluate governance, risk management, and ESG performance in unfamiliar operating environments.

Due Diligence Burden

Bespoke assessments for each opportunity create delays and inconsistency. Credit committees face variable-quality information across deals.

Missed Opportunity

Creditworthy companies struggle to demonstrate bankability. Well-run operations remain underlent while weaker borrowers absorb lender attention.
